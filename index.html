Learn HEX Math is written by Robert Sesek. I wrote it when learning X86 assembly and was finding that my inexperience with hexadecimal math was slowing me down. I remember in early elementary school, our teachers made us do math tables for time, and so I decided to recreate that experience online for doing HEX math.
